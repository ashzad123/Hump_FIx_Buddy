Back Hump Correction Guide

The back hump, also known as kyphosis, is a condition where the spine curves forward, leading to a hunched posture. Regular exercises can help correct this condition, and YWTL poses are particularly effective in strengthening the back and improving posture.

YWTL Poses

  • T-Pose: Strengthens the lower trapezius and rhomboids, which play a crucial role in maintaining an upright posture.
  • L-Pose: Targets the lower back and lumbar region, helping to stabilize the spine and reduce the curvature.

By performing these poses regularly, you can gradually correct your posture and alleviate the symptoms of a back hump.
